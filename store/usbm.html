Blue Motel

I drew this from inside this cafe with large windows. This old abandoned motel is on Fremont Street, Las Vegas, Nevada. It has bold color contrast ans is a plesant view from Public Us.

I drew this with a black, ballpoint pen in my baronfig sketchbook. Then I went over that drawing with Sharpie fine point permenant markers.
