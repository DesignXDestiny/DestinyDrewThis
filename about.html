About

Art by Destiny Barrett.
On this site I am showing you various works inspired by observation, imagination, memory, often a combination. Check out my work, how it is made, or decorate your space.

I love to draw. Always have. It would be great to have your feedback. I value constructive critisizm as a way to help me lean and grow faster. I want to share my art for the chance of making a connection.

My artwork is avalible for sale as limited edition prints (reproductions). Each one is embeleshed signed by the artist, you have the option of having it matted and then is shipped. Each print is unique. Each piece of art is a unique experience that can transform the feeling in a space.
